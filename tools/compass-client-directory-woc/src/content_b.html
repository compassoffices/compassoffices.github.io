House Rules客戶守則

These House Rules form an integral part of the Terms of Business of your agreement with Compass. They keep the centre a comfortable, professional and safe place to work for everyone.本《客戶守則》是您與 Compass 之間協議《商務條款》的組成部分，旨在讓中心對每一位客戶而言，都是舒適、專業而安全的工作場所。

Definitions定義

Capitalized terms in these House Rules have, where not otherwise defined, the same meaning as in your Agreement. References to "common areas" include lounges, pantries, corridors, and sitting and reception areas. References to the "Client's staff" include the Client's officers, directors, employees, shareholders, partners, agents, representatives, contractors, customers, guests and visitors.除另行規定外，本《客戶守則》中引號內的術語與《協議》中的含義相同。文中提及的所有「公共區域」包括休息室、茶水間、走廊以及休息和接待區。文中提及的「其僱員」包括客戶的高級職員、董事、僱員、股東、合作夥伴、代理人、代表、承包商、客戶、客人和訪客。

Etiquette & Safety禮儀與安全

  1. Compass takes pride in providing a great workplace for all our clients. To maintain a comfortable, pleasant and professional environment, the Client and the Client's staff must:
    1. Observe business and professional dress codes at all times while in the business centre;
    2. Keep noise levels to a minimum in offices and common areas;
    3. Use common areas only with Compass's consent and keep them in order;
    4. Refrain from conducting open business activities, meetings or gatherings in common areas, and from conducting meetings or gatherings with third parties in offices;
    5. Use lounge and pantry facilities for their intended purposes and keep them clean after use;
    6. Not allow animals or livestock to enter the business centre other than verified or registered service animals; and
    7. Not block, cover or write on any windows or glass in the business centre.
  2. Please leave common areas and meeting rooms as you found them — returning dirty cutlery and dishware to the pantry, not leaving personal possessions behind, cleaning up after yourselves, and advising Compass as soon as possible of any messes or spills you cannot clean up yourselves.
  3. Compass aims to provide a safe place for all clients. Please familiarize yourselves with the floor plan and the fire exits nearest your workplace. To ensure safety and security, the Client and the Client's staff must:
    1. Keep corridor, exit and connecting doors closed at all times;
    2. Not leave office spaces unattended without locking all doors;
    3. Not leave property unattended in common areas; and
    4. Follow all directions given by Compass staff or building management regarding the safety of the building and business centre.
  4. The Client should attempt to conserve energy by switching off lights and other electrical apparatus when leaving the office.
