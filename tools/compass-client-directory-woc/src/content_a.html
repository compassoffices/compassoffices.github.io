Security保安

Building security personnel patrol the centre regularly. If you notice anything suspicious, please tell any Compass staff member right away. For emergencies during non-office hours, contact building management at +852 2543 1117 or our emergency number +852 3182 7346.大廈保安人員定期巡邏中心。如發現任何可疑情況，請即通知任何 Compass 職員。非辦公時間如有緊急事故，請致電大廈管理處 +852 2543 1117 或我們的緊急熱線 +852 3182 7346。

In Case of Fire遇有火警

Dial ‘0’ to reach reception immediately, or call building management at +852 2543 1117, or Emergency Services on 999. Tell the operator the exact location of the fire. When leaving, follow the emergency exit signs and do not use the elevators (see the Evacuation Plan). If you cannot leave your room, keep the door closed and tell the operator where you are.請即按「0」聯絡接待處，或致電大廈管理處 +852 2543 1117，或緊急服務 999。請告知接線員火警的確實位置。離開時請依照緊急出口指示，切勿使用電梯（見疏散圖）。如無法離開房間，請關好門並告知接線員您的位置。

Building operating hours: Mon–Fri 07:00–20:00 · Sat 07:00–14:00 · Sun & public holidays closed.視乎地點，大廈管理處可能要求您於大堂登記或使用大廈門卡，方可於辦公時間後進入。大廈營業時間：週一至五 07:00–20:00 · 週六 07:00–14:00 · 週日及公眾假期休息。

Access Card門卡

All entrances and office units have security locks; an access card is required throughout. One card is issued per staff member, up to the number of workstations in your room. Requesting more cards than workstations adds a recurring monthly Habitat Membership fee for common-facility use. A refundable deposit applies per card — returned in good order at check-out. Lost or damaged cards are replaced at the listed price.所有出入口及辦公室單位均設保安鎖，進出須使用門卡。每位員工發一張門卡，數量上限為房間的工作位數量。若申請數量超過工作位，將就共用設施使用收取每月經常性 Habitat 會籍費用。每張門卡須繳付可退還按金，退租時完好歸還可獲退還。門卡遺失或損壞，按列明價格補發。
