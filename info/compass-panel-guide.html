Compass Offices — Digital Panel Operation Handbook

Purpose

This handbook is for Compass Offices staff to manage and update company listings on digital panels. Please follow the steps and do not share access with anyone outside Compass Offices.

How to Edit Company Information

Step 1: Visit the Dashboard

Go to: https://compassoffices.github.io/tools/digital-panel.html

Step 2: Select Location

Select the city, building, and level from the dashboard.

Step 3: Click Level

Click the "LEVEL" button (e.g., LEVEL 18) to open the editable Google Sheet.

Step 4: Edit Information

Update your company information directly in the Google Sheet. The panel will update automatically.

Anyone with the Google Sheet link can edit. Do not share the link externally.

Panel Behavior

Auto Refresh

The panel refreshes every 400 seconds (6 minutes 40 seconds).

Reconnection Icons

If the panel loses Wi-Fi or power, icons will appear at the top right. Reconnection happens automatically.

Manual Refresh

Once Wi-Fi reconnects, the two top-right icons disappear. Tap any link on the panel to refresh manually.

Power Management

Sleep Mode

Use the power button on the back of the panel to put it in sleep mode after hours. Turn it back on when needed.

Security Notice

This system is for internal Compass Offices staff only. Do not share dashboard or sheet access externally.
